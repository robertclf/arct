About Arctan, Inc.

Stay on the edge

Arctan, Inc. is a small C-corporation based in Arlington, VA., performing cutting-edge R&D for a select group of leading companies and government clients. We provide analytic augmentation and software development for risk analysis and impact assessment. The subject matter is often considered difficult to model, but has significant effects on business.

Our service is to rapidly develop, prove, and implement novel capabilities for use by our clients. These components offer measurable advantages in situational awareness and decision-making under uncertainty.

We are a fully independent company. We are able to rapidly deploy almost anywhere on the globe to accomplish analytic studies, trusted representation, prototyping and engineering.

What we do

Cutting-Edge Technology

A theme throughout our work is the unique 21st century problem of information overload.


Our integrated innovation toolkit leverages novel frameworks for human-machine cooperation combined with a lean approach of highly modular technologies, both software and hardware. We have successfully applied this ansatz to tackle complex problems which lie at the intersction of both human cognition and machine bandwidth limitations.

Technical fields of expertise include artificial intelligence, machine learning, telecommunications, data analytics, spectral analysis and game theory.
